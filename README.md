# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard | Excel Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This project showcases an interactive **Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard** developed in **Microsoft Excel**. The dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business insights through dynamic visualizations, KPI tracking, and interactive filtering.

The dashboard enables users to analyze sales performance, customer footfall, product category contributions, store-wise performance, and customer ordering behavior. It is designed to support data-driven decision-making for coffee shop operations and business growth.

## 📊 Dashboard Components

### 1️⃣ Quantity Ordered Based on Hours

This line chart analyzes customer ordering behavior throughout the day.

#### Key Insights:
- Peak order volume occurs between **8 AM and 10 AM**
- Morning hours contribute significantly to total sales
- Order volume gradually decreases during later hours

---

### 2️⃣ Category-wise Sales Distribution

This pie chart displays the percentage contribution of each product category to total sales.

#### Categories Included:
- Coffee
- Tea
- Bakery
- Drinking Chocolate
- Coffee Beans
- Loose Tea
- Packaged Chocolate
- Flavours

#### Key Insights:
- Coffee contributes the highest share of total revenue
- Tea is the second-largest revenue-generating category
- Specialty products contribute a smaller portion of overall sales

---

### 3️⃣ Order Size Distribution

This visualization shows customer preferences for drink sizes.

#### Categories:
- Large
- Regular
- Small
- Not Defined

#### Key Insights:
- Regular and Large sizes account for the majority of orders
- Small-sized orders contribute a smaller share

---

### 4️⃣ Store-wise Sales & Footfall Analysis

This chart compares customer footfall and sales performance across store locations.

#### Store Locations:
- Astoria
- Hell's Kitchen
- Lower Manhattan

#### Key Insights:
- Hell's Kitchen generates the highest sales revenue
- Astoria records strong customer traffic
- Lower Manhattan remains highly competitive in sales performance

---

### 5️⃣ Top 5 Products Based on Sales

This chart highlights the highest revenue-generating products.

#### Top Products:
1. Barista Espresso
2. Brewed Chai Tea
3. Hot Chocolate
4. Gourmet Brewed Coffee
5. Brewed Black Tea

#### Key Insights:
- Barista Espresso is the best-selling product
- Tea-based products contribute significantly to revenue

---

### 6️⃣ Orders by Weekday

This chart shows customer order volume throughout the week.

#### Key Insights:
- Friday receives the highest number of orders
- Monday and Thursday also show strong performance
- Saturday experiences comparatively lower order volume
